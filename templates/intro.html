IF INFORMED

What will happen?

On the next page, we will ask you a few questions about your age, gender, income, race, and so on.

On the basis of data from the American National Election Studies (ANES), we will then estimate how you likely would have voted, if you were fully informed.

Why?

Most of us think it wortwhile to listen to their more informed friends’ opinions. We think: if this person is 'quite like me' and really knows what’s going on, then their opinion is worth hearing.

You can think of this website as telling you what a particularly knowledgeable friend thinks about a number of political issues—where that friend just happens to be a highly-informed version of you.

Who are we?

If Informed is a collaboration between Kristoffer Ahlstrom-Vij, a researcher at Birkbeck, University of London, who works on the role of knowledge in politics, and Alfred Malmros, founder of the think-tank Early Studies.
